I am a Ph.D. student at the [State Key Laboratory of Blockchain and Data Security, Zhejiang University](https://www.zju.edu.cn/).
My research interests lie in software engineering, with a focus on software refactoring, software migration, and vulnerability impact analysis.

I am fortunate to be advised by [Professor Xiaohu Yang](https://person.zju.edu.cn/xiaohu), [Professor Xin Xia](https://xin-xia.github.io/), and [Associate Professor Xing Hu](https://xing-hu.github.io/) at Zhejiang University.
